about me

Hello! I'm Marta Teva, a telecommunications engineer and passionate about the world of timing and synchronisation.

Right now, I work at a company where I do just that: make things happen at the exact moment they need to happen (and believe me, it's more complicated than it sounds).

Although I love my job, I also have my own personal projects that allow me to experiment, learn new technologies, and keep alive the curiosity that brought me to engineering in the first place.

I like to mix the technical with the creative, and I'm always looking for new challenges that take me out of my comfort zone.

aboutmy journey

My journey in telecommunications has been long, intense and very enriching.

I first studied Telecommunications Engineering, where I discovered my fascination with how systems communicate and how technology can coordinate processes in ways that previously seemed impossible.

I then went on to do a related master's degree, delving even deeper into complex systems, critical infrastructures and, of course, synchronisation and timing.

Each year was a mixture of theory, practice and countless hours in the laboratory, working on projects and drinking coffee, but all of that helped me become the professional I am today.

about vision

Looking ahead, I have a very clear goal: to start my own business in the telecommunications sector.

I want to build something of my own, a project where I can apply everything I have learned and bring my most personal ideas to life in a real-world setting.

I am excited by the idea of contributing something new, innovative and useful to the sector, and I know that this career path is taking me step by step towards that goal.

I am convinced that with effort, perseverance and a lot of technological curiosity, I will achieve it.
